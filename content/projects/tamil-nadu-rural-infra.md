### Project Overview
A comprehensive rural development initiative aimed at improving connectivity and sanitation in Tiruchirappalli district.

### Scope of Work
* **Roads:** Laying of 15km of all-weather roads.
* **Water:** Installation of overhead tanks and pipeline networks.
* **Community:** Construction of 3 multi-purpose community halls.
